Grid Summary Service (GSS)
The Grid Summary Service collects information about caGrid and the services deployed on the grid. This data is made available through various mobile-optimized interfaces as well as Application Programming Interfaces (Java, REST, WS).

The caGrid iPhone App will make use of GSS and caB2B to allow for querying across the grid from your iPhone or iPad.
